What Is It?

PixelCons are collectible tokens featuring minimalist pixel art. It was created as a way for people to easily make collectibles within a shared ecosystem and showcase incredible art. Every PixelCon is unique and it's ownership is secured on the Ethereum blockchain. Feel free to browse other artists creations or make your own.

Why Minimalist Pixel Art?

An 8x8 grid with 16 colors fits perfectly into the 256bit ID size that Ethereum based tokens use. This means that the artwork data also serves as a PixelCons unique ID. If you think you couldn't possibly create anything meaningful with these restrictions, then definitely check out some of the impressive art already created.

Isn't This Just a CryptoKitties Clone?

Although CryptoKitties was an inspiration, PixelCons isn't just some copycat.
  • PixelCons isn't really a game, just a collectibles ecosystem
  • No developer fees, just pay for gas
  • The minor admin role only effects small read-only data
  • Everything is done from a static website (no hidden proprietary systems)
  • You don't have to buy PixelCons to start, just create your own instead!
Open Source
