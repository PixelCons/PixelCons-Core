What Is It?

PixelCons are collectible NFTs featuring minimalist pixel art. This open platform was started in 2018 to allow any artist to easily make digital collectibles within a shared ecosystem. Every PixelCon is unique and it's ownership is secured on the Ethereum blockchain.
